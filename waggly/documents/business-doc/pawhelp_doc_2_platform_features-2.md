### Core Features
PawHelp provides a comprehensive, interconnected set of features designed to facilitate trusted, transparent, and community-driven assistance for animals in need. The platform serves pet owners, shelters, volunteers, donors, service providers, and sponsors alike, providing essential functionality to save lives and build a global animal welfare ecosystem.

#### Help Request Management
- Multi-step, guided creation process with clear instructions.
- Mandatory verification of medical documentation to prevent fraud.
- Escrow-based fundraising goal setup ensuring donor trust.
- Photo uploads with quality control suggestions.
- Case descriptions with rich text editor for detailed storytelling.
- Flexible visibility and contact preferences for case creators.
- AI-driven assistance to suggest optimal case formatting and structure.
- Progress tracking dashboard for organizers.
- Editable case updates to inform supporters.
- Case closure and success story functionality.
- Admin oversight for case approval and safety.

#### Donation System with Escrow & Transparency
- Secure, multi-currency donations held in escrow until the fundraising goal is met, ensuring donors' funds are only released when the intended help is achievable.
- Full transparency of progress, including real-time updates on total donations received, remaining amount needed, and number of contributors.
- Detailed donation breakdown available for each case, highlighting top contributors, average donation amounts, and contribution trends.
- Fund usage tracking available after payout to show recipients' declared use of the funds, with option to upload follow-up reports or success stories.
- Automatic currency conversion based on real-time exchange rates with clear indication to donors of converted amounts.
- Preset donation amounts designed to encourage participation, with AI suggesting optimal donation tiers based on case urgency and goal size.
- Custom donation entry option for flexibility, allowing donors to contribute any amount within platform and regional limits.
- Recurring donation options, including weekly, monthly, or custom schedules, with pause or cancellation functionality accessible to donors at any time.
- Anonymous donation functionality protecting donor privacy while still contributing to progress.
- AI-powered fraud detection with real-time transaction risk scoring, anomaly detection, and suspicious behavior alerts sent to admin panel.
- Manual transaction reviews triggered for flagged donations, with temporary fund holds until verification.
- Donation history tracking for each user with clear summaries of total contributions, supported cases, and badges earned.
- Leaderboard and "Top Donors" section in each case, highlighting significant contributors (respecting anonymity settings).
- Recognition of top donors within the community through profile badges, "Heroes Nearby" visibility, and periodic feature highlights.
- Admin-controlled fund release process, requiring manual verification that fundraising goals are met and case documentation remains valid.
- Automatic notifications to case creators and donors upon successful fund release.
- Tax-compliant receipt generation (where applicable), with downloadable receipts available in user accounts.
- Future expansion planned to include integration with verified external escrow providers for regulated markets and advanced financial audit trails.

#### Blood Donation Network
- Comprehensive pet blood donor registration system.
- AI-powered compatibility matching (blood type, size, location, health factors).
- Urgent blood donation alerts based on proximity.
- Veterinary clinic integration for verification.
- Educational content on pet blood donation and eligibility.
- Donor status management for availability tracking.
- Appointment scheduling and notifications.
- Recognition of active blood donors in "Heroes Nearby."
- Health record linkage for pets (future phase).

#### Community & Social Features
- Verified user profiles for pet owners, animal lovers, and donors.
- Groups for shared interests, local communities, and cause-based collaboration.
- Secure messaging (private and group chats).
- Public recognition for donors, helpers, and active community members.
- Success stories and status updates for cases.
- AI-powered content moderation for safety.
- Commenting and social sharing on cases.
- Community badges, leaderboards, and achievements.
- "Heroes Nearby" map highlighting top contributors.
- Event creation and participation functionality.
- Partner and sponsor visibility in community areas.

#### Geographic Targeting & Location-Based Search
- Regional filters for browsing help requests and services.
- Nearby donor, helper, and blood donor discovery tools.
- Map-based case and resource browsing.
- Local community highlights and newsfeeds.
- Location-based urgency alerts.
- Customizable search radius.
- Region-specific content adaptation.
- Location privacy settings for users.

#### Service Marketplace & Bookings
- Verified listings for clinics, pet stores, shelters, groomers, and volunteers.
- Service reviews, ratings, and trust indicators.
- Direct booking with service providers.
- AI-driven service recommendations based on location and needs.
- Partner and sponsor promotional opportunities.
- Booking management and reminders.
- Marketplace search filtering by service type, location, and ratings.
- Business profile visibility within the community.
- Integration of sponsor offers and discounts.

#### AI Features
- Smart Case Suggestions to increase donation success.
- AI Help Request Creation Assistant for optimal case formatting.
- AI-Powered Content Moderation for text, images, and documents.
- AI Fraud Detection & Behavioral Risk Scoring.
- Blood Donor Compatibility Matching with real-time suggestions.
- Personalized content recommendations based on user behavior.
- AI-driven content translation and localization.

#### Multi-language & Localization Support
- Multi-language interface with region-specific adaptations.
- AI-assisted content translation for cases and user-generated content.
- Automatic currency conversion and regional payment options.
