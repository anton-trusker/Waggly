### AI Moderation & Human Oversight
- AI tools assist with:
  - Content flagging (text, images).
  - Fraud detection & risk scoring.
  - Blood donor compatibility suggestions.
  - Smart case recommendations.
- Final moderation decisions rest with human admins.
- All AI actions are logged and traceable.

---

### Role Evolutions Allowed
- Pet Owner to:
  - Blood Donor.
  - Verified Service Provider.
- Private Individual to:
  - Blood Donor.
  - Verified Service Provider (if applicable).
- Business/Non-Commercial roles require verification.
- Sponsors may increase platform visibility based on contributions.

---

### Multi-Level Verification for Public Profiles
- Business, Non-Commercial, and Sponsor profiles require admin approval.
- Help requests require medical documentation verification.
- Blood donor registrations require health screening validation.

### Guest Limitations & Registration Incentives
- Browsing allowed.
- Donations, participation, and profile creation gated behind registration.
- Contextual prompts encourage sign-up at key interaction points.

### Data Security & Privacy Controls by Role
- Field-level visibility restrictions based on role.
- Private information hidden from guests.
- Strict data handling for sensitive fields (medical, financial).
- Compliance with GDPR and regional privacy laws.

### User Trust Scores & Reputation System (Future Phase)
- Transparency-enhancing public trust scores.
- Calculated from:
  - Verification status.
  - Donation history.
  - Community participation.
  - Report history and moderation outcomes.
- Scores displayed selectively to promote safety.

---

*Waggli's role-based permissions and access control system ensures platform security, trust, and user-appropriate functionality while providing the flexibility to grow and evolve within the animal welfare community.*
