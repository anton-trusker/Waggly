# Waggli Operational Plan
**Building a Scalable, AI-Driven Organization**

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** January 2026  
**Status:** Operational Roadmap

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Waggli operates as an **AI-Native Organization**. By leveraging AI for 80% of routine tasks—coding, customer support, localization, and content generation—we maintain a lean headcount while scaling to millions of users. This capital efficiency allows us to invest more in growth and features.

---

## 2. Organizational Structure

### 2.1 The Lean Team (Year 1)
To support 15,000 users and €500k ARR, we need just **4-5 Full-Time Employees (FTEs)**.

#### **A. Leadership**
*   **CEO/Founder:** Strategy, Fundraising, Partnerships, Product Vision.
*   **CTO/Co-Founder:** Technical Architecture, AI Pipeline, Security.

#### **B. Product & Engineering**
*   **Founding Engineer (Full Stack):** React Native + Supabase.
*   **AI Engineer:** LLM fine-tuning, OCR optimization, Data pipeline.
*   *Note: We rely on "AI Pair Programmers" (Cursor/Copilot) effectively doubling output.*

#### **C. Growth & Operations**
*   **Growth Marketing Lead:** Paid UA, Influencer management, Content strategy.
*   **Customer Success / Ops (Part-Time):** Handling Tier 2 support (Tier 1 is AI).

### 2.2 Scaling Plan (Year 2)
As we hit 80k users, we expand to:
*   **Partnerships Manager:** Dedication to Vet/Insurance relationships.
*   **Community Manager:** Managing social feeds and events.
*   **QA/Mobile Engineer:** Ensuring app stability across devices.

## 3. Operational Workflows

### 3.1 Customer Support
**Philosophy:** "Zero-Touch Resolution."
1.  **Level 1 (AI Bot):** Intercepts 80% of queries ("How do I add a vaccine?", "Reset password").
2.  **Level 2 (Human):** Complex billing issues, bug reports.
3.  **SLA:** <60s for AI response, <24h for Human response (Pro users <4h).

### 3.2 Content & Localization
**Process:**
1.  **Creation:** English core content (Blogs, App Strings).
2.  **AI Translation:** Automated pipeline translates to DE, NL, FR.
3.  **Human Review:** Freelance native speakers (4-hour contract/week) verify cultural nuance.
4.  **Publish:** Simultaneous rollout across all markets.

### 3.3 Data & Privacy Operations
*   **GDPR Requests:** Automated portal for "Download My Data" and "Right to be Forgotten."
*   **Security Audits:** Quarterly automated scans + Annual 3rd party penetration test.

## 5. Risk Management

### 5.1 Regulatory Risks (GDPR & Data)
*   **Risk:** Managing sensitive health data across borders.
*   **Mitigation:** 
    *   Hosting in EU (Frankfurt).
    *   Strict encryption.
    *   Appointment of Data Protection Officer (External consultant).

### 5.2 Platform Dependency
*   **Risk:** Reliance on OpenAI/Google for AI features.
*   **Mitigation:** 
    *   Model Agnostic Architecture (can switch to Anthropic/Llama).
    *   Local OCR models for offline capability.

### 5.3 Vet Pushback
*   **Risk:** Vets viewing Waggli as "Doctor Google" (misinformation).
*   **Mitigation:**
    *   Clear Disclaimers: "Not medical advice."
    *   Pro-Vet Features: "Share with Vet" functionality builds trust.
