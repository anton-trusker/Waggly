## The Problem We Solve

### 1. Scattered Health Records Create Dangerous Care Gaps
- Pet owners juggle 3-5 different platforms plus paper records
- Vets can't access patient history in emergencies
- Critical information lost when changing clinics
- Manual tracking leads to missed vaccines and medication errors

### 2. No Digital Infrastructure for Modern Pet Care
- 340 million pets still rely on paper passports
- Manual data entry creates massive friction and abandonment
- No intelligent reminders or preventive care guidance
- Fragmented ecosystem with no single source of truth

### 3. Pet Humanization Demands Better Solutions
- 71% of European pet owners consider pets as family members
- €121 billion market growing 5% annually
- 20% veterinary staffing shortage increasing wait times
- Owners expect the same digital health tools they have for themselves

---

## Our Solution

**Waggly is the conversational AI digital pet passport that works globally from day one.**

### Zero-Effort Health Management

Instead of forcing manual data entry, Waggly uses conversational AI:

| Feature | How It Works |
|---------|--------------|
| **Talk to Waggly** | Natural conversation about pet's vet visit, AI extracts and organizes data |
| **Photograph Documents** | OCR automatically extracts data from prescriptions, test results, vaccine records |
| **Record Conversations** | AI transcribes vet conversations into structured health records |
| **Proactive Notifications** | AI health notifications before problems arise |
