## Customization Guidelines

**Before Sending Any Email:**

1. **Personalize Opening**: Reference something specific about the fund (recent deal, portfolio company, thesis focus)
2. **Adjust Traction Data**: Use actual metrics (or "pre-launch" language if no traction yet)
3. **Tailor "Why You" Section**: Explain specific fit with that investor's thesis
4. **Update Attached Materials**: Ensure deck/exec summary version is latest
5. **Proofread**: Check investor name, fund name, company names for accuracy
6. **Timing**: Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient's timezone for best open rates

## Email Tracking Best Practices

**Tools to Use:**
- **Mailtrack** or **HubSpot Sales** (email open tracking)
- **Calendly** (easy scheduling links)
- **Docsend** (track deck views)

**Follow-Up Cadence:**
- Day 0: Send initial email
- Day 3: Follow up if no response (brief, friendly nudge)
- Day 7: Final follow-up
- After 7 days: Move to "low priority," focus on other investors

**Response Rate Expectations:**
- Cold outreach: 10-20% response rate
- Warm intros: 60-80% response rate
- Applications (like Pawsible): 100% response (rejection or interest)
