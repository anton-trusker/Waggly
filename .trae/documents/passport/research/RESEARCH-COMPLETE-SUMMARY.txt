## üí° KEY CALCULATIONS

### Vaccination Compliance
```
Valid Vaccines / Required Vaccines √ó 100 = Compliance %

Where:
- Valid = not expired
- Required = based on species, age, breed, location
```

### Body Condition Score (BCS)
```
1-3: Underweight ‚Üí Increase calories
4-5: Ideal ‚Üí Maintain
6-7: Overweight ‚Üí Reduce 10-20%, increase exercise
8-9: Obese ‚Üí Veterinary intervention required
```

### Preventive Care Score
```
Start: 100 points
- Vaccination issues: deduct (1 - compliance%) √ó 40
- Missed wellness visits: deduct 15-30
- Dental health: deduct 7-15
- Parasite prevention: deduct 7-8
Result: max(0, min(100, score))
```
