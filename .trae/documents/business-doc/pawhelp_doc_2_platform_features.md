**PawHelp Document 2: Platform Features & Functional Specification**

---

### Core Features

#### Help Request Management
- Multi-step, guided creation process.
- Mandatory verification of medical documentation.
- Escrow-based fundraising goal setup.
- Photo uploads and case descriptions.
- Flexible visibility and contact preferences.

#### Donation System with Escrow & Transparency
- Secure donations held in escrow until fundraising goal is met.
- Full transparency of progress and fund usage.
- Multi-currency support with automatic conversion.
- Preset and custom donation amounts.
- Recurring donation options.
- Anonymous donation functionality.
- AI fraud detection & monitoring.

#### Blood Donation Network
- Pet blood donor registration.
- AI-powered compatibility matching (blood type, location, size).
- Urgent blood donation alerts.
- Integration with veterinary clinics.
- Educational content on pet blood donation.

#### Community & Social Features
- User profiles for pet owners and animal lovers.
- Groups for shared interests and local communities.
- Messaging (private and group chats).
- Public recognition for donors and helpers.
- Success stories and case updates.
- AI moderation for safety and content quality.

#### Geographic Targeting & Location-Based Search
- Regional filters for help requests.
- Nearby donor and helper discovery.
- Map-based case browsing.
- Local community highlights.

#### Service Marketplace & Bookings
- Verified listings for clinics, pet stores, groomers.
- Service reviews and ratings.
- Direct booking with service providers.
- AI-driven service recommendations.
- Partner and sponsor promotional opportunities.

#### AI Features
- Smart Case Suggestions to potential donors.
- AI Help Request Creation Assistant.
- AI-Powered Content Moderation (text, images).
- AI Fraud Detection & Risk Scoring.
- Blood Donor Compatibility Matching.
- Personalized content recommendations.
- AI-driven Content Translation & Localization.

#### Multi-language & Localization Support
- Multi-language interface with region-specific adaptations.
- AI-assisted content translation.
- Currency conversion and regional payment options.
- Localized date, time, and address formats.

### User Flows

#### Pet Owner Journey
- Registration & verification.
- Case creation.
- Fundraising management.
- Community participation.
- Donor or service provider evolution (if applicable).

#### Donor/Helper Journey
- Browsing help requests.
- Secure donation process.
- Offering blood donation or physical help.
- Receiving updates and recognition.

#### Blood Donor Journey
- Pet registration.
- Compatibility matching.
- Appointment booking.
- Donor status management.

#### Pet Lover Community Journey
- Registration.
- Joining groups.
- Messaging and sharing.
- Participating in events.
- Leaving reviews.

#### Partner/Business/Shelter Flows
- Profile creation.
- Verification process.
- Service listing and management.
- Booking handling.
- Community collaboration.

#### Admin Flows (Full Oversight)
- Platform configuration.
- User, content, and case moderation.
- Business and shelter approvals.
- AI moderation management.
- Financial and integration oversight.
- Reporting and analytics access.

---

### User Onboarding & Education
- Step-by-step guidance for all user types.
- Contextual help throughout flows.
- Educational resources for donations, blood donations, and responsible pet ownership.

---

### Incentive & Recognition System
- Donor and helper badges.
- Featured community heroes.
- Case success stories.
- Progress-based recognition.
- Sponsor visibility opportunities.

*PawHelp's feature set ensures an intuitive, secure, and trustworthy experience for all user groups, enhancing the platform's ability to deliver real-world animal welfare impact at scale.*
